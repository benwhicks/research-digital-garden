#thought 
A [[graphical-causal-model\|Systems Map]] can act as a way for experts from relevant domains ([[domain-experts\|context experts]]) communicate their systems knowledge to [[data-experts\|data experts]]. In this way they act as a [[boundary-objects\|boundary object]] #getref between experts, a visual tools that is simple enough for both parties to understand but formal enough to inform modelling the system.
